Cancellation Policy for Johnson Learning Platform

At Johnson Learning Platform, we are committed to providing a valuable and seamless learning experience. Please review our cancellation policy, which outlines the procedures in case you need to cancel a Platform enrolment or subscription plan.

Cancellation Policy:

1. Platform Enrolment:

- Users can cancel their enrolment in a Platform within 2 days from the date of purchase.

- To cancel, contact our support team through the platform or email us at care@johnsonpublications.in.

- After the 2-day period, Platform enrolments are considered final.

2. Subscription Plans:

- Any subscription plans can be cancelled at any time.

- To cancel a subscription, users can contact us directly to process their cancellation.

Refund Policy:

1. Non-Refundable:

- Please note that Johnson Learning Platform does not offer refunds for Platform enrolments or subscription plans.

2. Cancellation Process:

- Users can still cancel enrolments or subscription plans using the procedures outlined above, even though refunds will not be provided.

Additional Notes:

1. Currency Conversion:

- All transactions and fees are processed in the currency originally selected during the purchase.

2. Non-Transferable:

- Platform enrolments and subscription plans are non-transferable.

3. Policy Changes:

- Johnson Learning Platform reserves the right to update or modify the cancellation policy. Users will be notified of any changes.

For any further clarification or assistance regarding cancellations, please contact our support team at care@johnsonpublications.in. We appreciate your understanding and thank you for choosing Johnson Learning Platform for your educational journey.
