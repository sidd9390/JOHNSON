Welcome to Johnson Learning Platform Help Centre

Getting Started:

1. Platform Enrolment:

- Create an Account: Begin by clicking "Sign Up," where you'll be prompted to enter your details. After completion, don't forget to verify your mobile number for seamless access to the platform.

- Troubleshooting: In case you encounter any issues during the account creation process or OTP’s, please wait for 5 minutes to receive the OTP. Should you still face challenges, our dedicated support team is just an email away to assist you promptly.

2. Dashboard Overview:

- Dive into the heart of your learning experience with the dashboard. Discover a user-friendly interface designed to enhance your navigation and interaction with course materials.

- Content: All the content has been developed based on the curriculum of Johnson Publications, and all the curriculum is highly recommended for the best growth of the child’s education sector, as all the courses and curriculum are designed such that all of the materials are designed as per the standards set by the Government of India.

Courses and Learning:

1. Enrolling in Courses:

- All the course materials are exclusively available for the schools/ clients who follow Johnson Publications curriculum. Empower yourself by browsing through a diverse range of courses. Enrolling is a breeze - simply click "Signup" on the home page to start learning courses of your choice. Access your assigned courses conveniently from the dashboard.

2. Progress Tracking:

- Take charge of your academic journey by monitoring your progress, grades, and completion status. Leverage analytics to gain insights that will contribute to your continuous improvement.

Managing Your Profile:

1. Profile Settings:

- Your profile is your online identity. Keep it updated by navigating to "Profile icon at the top and clicking on Account." Ensure accurate information for effective communication within the learning community.

2. Notifications:

- Stay in the loop with timely updates and announcements. All the notifications from the platform are communicated with the end users through the mobile number that they provide during the signup process.

Troubleshooting and FAQs:

2. Technical Support:
