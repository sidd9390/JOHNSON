Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I create an account on Johnson Learning Platform?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button on the homepage. Follow the prompts to provide the required information. If you are having difficulty with the signing-up process, you can contact the school to help you out with the process.

2. What age group is Johnson Learning Platform designed for?

Our platform is intended for users aged 3 and above.

3. How can I reset my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email to reset your password.

4. Can I access courses for free?

All of the digital content available on this platform is available for a reasonable annual fee. However, schools and institutions that follow the Johnson Publications curriculum may get an annual subscription at no extra cost to their users, under set conditions.

5. How do I report an issue or error on the platform?

If you encounter any issues or errors, please contact our support team at it@johnsonpublications.in. Provide details about the problem, and we'll work to resolve it promptly.

6. Are my payments secure on Johnson Learning Platform?

Yes, we take the security of your payments seriously. All transactions are encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways to ensure your financial information is protected.

7. Can I download course materials for offline access?

Currently, our platform does not support the downloading of course materials for offline access. However, you can access the materials online at any time.

8. How often are courses updated?

We strive to keep our courses updated with the latest information. However, the frequency of updates may vary for each course. Check the course description for specific details.

9. What happens if I violate the terms of use?

Violations of our terms of use may result in the suspension or termination of your account. Refer to our Terms and Conditions for more details on acceptable use.

10. How is my personal information handled on Johnson Learning Platform?

We take user privacy seriously. Please review our Privacy Policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

11. Can I share my account with others?

Sharing accounts is not allowed. Each user should have their own account. Account sharing may result in the suspension of your account.

13. Is there a refund policy for the subscription fees?

All of the paid subscriptions are generally non-refundable. Please contact our support team for specific details.

12. How can I stay informed about updates and changes to the platform?

Stay informed about updates and changes by regularly checking our "Legal" section, which includes our Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions, and Disclaimer.
